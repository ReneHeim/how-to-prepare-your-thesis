# Master Thesis Colloquium Preparation Guide

## Presentation Structure
- Introduction
- Motivation/Problem Statement
- Objectives
- Methods/Approach
- Results
- Discussion
- Conclusion/Future Work

## Tips for Success
- Clear slides; one main idea per slide
- Visuals over text
- Rehearse with audience
- Practice Q&A

## Day-Of Checklist
- Arrive 30 min early
- Test all equipment
- Bring water
- Speak clearly, make eye contact
- Welcome questions as chances to elaborate

*Good luck on your thesis examination!*
